MINUS ONE SCREEN

Minus One Screen is my first project in the intelligent design area. Minus One Screen is usually located on the left-hand side of the desktop. And the users can have a look at the recommendations including food orders, reminders, and calendars by swiping right.

My major responsibility in this project is user research and prototype design.

What is Minus One Screen?

Almost all mobile phones have a Minus One Screen, yet the contents will be different based on their design strategies. While some of the Minus One Screen display the panel for smart home, some provide quick access to the app function.

In this project, the Minus One Screen plays the role of smart recommendations and instant services.

What is Minus One Screen Smart Card

The phone is like a small box. When we unfold the phone, we can see that the main screen is surrounded by various kinds of messages. And minus one screen is one of the surfaces of the box which provides the intelligently recommended messages.

The smart card plays the most important role in the minus one screen. And since the smart card will be intelligently arranged in different orders, we should develop a design guideline so that the cards can be displayed more consistently.

Design the Minus One Screen

We developed a basic framework to design the smart card, which consists of four parts.

Part 1: Business

We summarised the different types of businesses that can be displayed as smart cards.

Part 2: Layer

Arrange the businesses in different layers and develop the basic structure.

Part 3: Module

After defining the overall structure, we develop the modules based on the structure.

Part 4: Element

We develop the details and the contents of the cards.
