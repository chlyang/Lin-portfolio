Yesterday Once More

Yesterday Once More is a project designed for people with dementia. Dementia is an overall term that describes a wide range of symptoms associated with a decline in memory or other thinking skills. With augmented reality technology, this project aims at postponing the development of dementia by helping them remember the past events.
