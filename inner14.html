SUPPORT APP REDESIGN

Background

Huawei Support is a pre-installed app in Huawei phone that assist the users to find that best official service.

Globally, there are so many Huawei Service Stores which provide supports and helps to the customers.

Yet behind these services, there are several issues such as low service efficiency and delay response.

The support app provides online service that may allow the customers to solve the problem by themselves, which eases the problem between the customers and the service stores.

Whenever there is an issue, the customers can go to Support App first to find the solution. Therefore the the Support App is like a virtual store that saves the customers much time.

Why we need to redesign the current app

Having a look at the current design, what can be seen is a bunch of functions waiting for the customers to choose, which, similar to the product shelves in the actual store, enhances the burden of making choice.

Design

After analyzing the pinpoints and the first stage researches, we redesigned the app with the idea of providing a warm and instructional service.

What is an instructional service? We analyze the current experience in Huawei Store and try to redeisgn the app based on the actual customer's experience.
