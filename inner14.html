SUPPORT APP REDESIGN

Background

Huawei Support is a pre-installed app in Huawei phone that assists the users to find that best official service.

Globally, there are so many Huawei Service Stores which provide supports and helps to the customers.

Yet after analyzing the trouble issues from the store, it is found that 81% of the issues could be solved by the customers themselves.

As a person busy working everyday, normally when there is a phone issue, they seldom have time to go to a store for repair.

Whenever there is an issue, the customers can go to the Support App first to find the solution. Therefore the Support App is like a virtual store that saves the customers much time.

Why we need to redesign the current app

Having a look at the current design, what can be seen is a bunch of functions waiting for the customers to choose, which, similar to the product shelves in the actual store, enhances the user’s burden of making choice.

Research

How to redesign the Support App and improve its experience for the customers? We need to understand the current user’s feelings about the current product.
